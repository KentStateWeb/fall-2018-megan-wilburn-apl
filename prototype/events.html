Paws to Taste

Date/Time

November 17, 2018

Description

Paws to Taste is being held November 17th at the Kent State University Hotel again this year and we couldn't be more excited! Mark your calendar or make your reservations now! It's going to be another great year including sampling wine and beer, heavy Hors d' oeuvres and raising much needed funds for Portage APL. Reservations are $55 per person before November 3rd and $60 after, and include 3 tasting tickets, food and fun! More tasting tickets will be available for purchase at the event. Bid on silent auction items like gift baskets, art, jewelry, gift certificates and more. The event wouldn't be complete without a 50/50 raffle and we will also have the Wine Pull again this year at $20 per bottle! Sponsorships are available and information will be posted soon. Space is limited so don't miss out. Must be 21 years and older to register.
